Bachelor of Computer Technology

The BCT is a four-year degree program that focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing. the program is develops core skill in multimedia in technology web mobile development and network/system administration. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Complience (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

the program prepares learnes with practical skills in multimedia technology, web development and network/system administration and supervisoral skills to manage an organizationsin these areas of competencies, engage in a start up business or pursue teaching career.
